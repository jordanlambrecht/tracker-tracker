# Tracker Tracker

Self-hosted dashboard for monitoring your private tracker stats over time.

## What is Tracker Tracker?

Tracker Tracker connects to your private tracker accounts via their APIs and records your stats (upload, download, ratio, buffer, seed count, etc.) over time. It supports UNIT3D, Gazelle, and GazelleGames platforms.

## Features

- **All your trackers in one place** — Upload, download, ratio, buffer, rank, and more across every site you're on
- **Historical charts** — See how your stats change over days, weeks, and months
- **qBittorrent dashboard** — Your torrents, seeds, and transfer speeds right next to your tracker stats
- **Discord alerts** — Get notified when your ratio drops, a tracker goes down, or you hit a rank milestone
